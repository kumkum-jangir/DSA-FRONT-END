Calculus Formulas

Limits

Limit of a function: \\( \lim_{x \to a} f(x) = L \\)

Example: Find the limit of f(x) = 2x + 3 as x approaches 1: \\( \lim_{x \to 1} (2x + 3) = 5 \\).

Derivatives

Derivative of xⁿ: \\( \frac{d}{dx} x^n = n x^{n-1} \\)
Derivative of sin(x): \\( \frac{d}{dx} \sin(x) = \cos(x) \\)
Derivative of cos(x): \\( \frac{d}{dx} \cos(x) = -\sin(x) \\)
Derivative of eˣ: \\( \frac{d}{dx} e^x = e^x \\)
Derivative of ln(x): \\( \frac{d}{dx} \ln(x) = \frac{1}{x} \\)

Integrals

Integral of xⁿ: \\( \int x^n \, dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C \\)
Integral of sin(x): \\( \int \sin(x) \, dx = -\cos(x) + C \\)
Integral of cos(x): \\( \int \cos(x) \, dx = \sin(x) + C \\)
Integral of eˣ: \\( \int e^x \, dx = e^x + C \\)
Integral of 1/x: \\( \int \frac{1}{x} \, dx = \ln |x| + C \\)

Chain Rule

\\( \frac{d}{dx} f(g(x)) = f'(g(x)) \cdot g'(x) \\)

Example: Find the derivative of f(x) = sin(2x): \\( \frac{d}{dx} \sin(2x) = 2 \cos(2x) \\).

Product Rule

\\( \frac{d}{dx} [u(x) \cdot v(x)] = u'(x) \cdot v(x) + u(x) \cdot v'(x) \\)

Example: Find the derivative of f(x) = x² * sin(x): \\( \frac{d}{dx} [x² \cdot \sin(x)] = 2x \cdot \sin(x) + x² \cdot \cos(x) \\).

Quotient Rule

\\( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{u(x)}{v(x)} \right) = \frac{v(x) \cdot u'(x) - u(x) \cdot v'(x)}{v(x)²} \\)

Example: Find the derivative of f(x) = (x²)/(cos(x)): \\( \frac{d}{dx} \left( \frac{x²}{\cos(x)} \right) = \frac{2x \cdot \cos(x) + x² \cdot \sin(x)}{\cos²(x)} \\).

Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

\\( \int_{a}^{b} f(x) \, dx = F(b) - F(a) \\), where F'(x) = f(x)

Example: Use the theorem to calculate the area under the curve of f(x) = x² from 0 to 2: \\( \int_0^2 x² \, dx = \left[ \frac{x³}{3} \right]_0^2 = \frac{8}{3} - 0 = \frac{8}{3} \\).
